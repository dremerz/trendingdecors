NAVIN HOUSING & PROPERTIES PVT. LTD.

THE HOME MAKERS WHO CARE

TAMIL NADU'S FIRST DEVELOPER TO SELL BY CARPET AREA*

"NAVIN’S believe that building homes is an art, and an exercise of intellect, careful precision, and passion. Since 1989, the motto of the organization has been to achieve the culmination of architectural brilliance and value-for-price in the projects. Over 120 projects dot Chennai's skyline that stand testimony to a passion called Navin’s; and the long journey that intertwines a cherished dream - to be the most trusted builder. Navin’s is the first developer in Chennai to receive the ISO 9001-2015 Certification , adhering to offering clear titles, excellent locations, strict adherence to rules and regulations, care for customer needs, and above all, ethical business practices.

At Navin’s our construction process goes through checks- over 1275 quality checks, ensuring the best quality. We are among the first developer to sell by carpet area in Tamilnadu. Our projects stand testimony to our quality, trust and our motto ‘We love our customers’. "
