A payment form is a web page that gives the option to add product details along with the option to collect user details. You can collect payments from multiple customers using the same Form, without any integration or coding. Share the Form URL with your customers on SMS, email, WhatsApp, other messaging apps, or embed it on your website and app and start collecting payments within minutes.
