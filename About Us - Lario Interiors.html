About us

We started from a humble beginning

Lario Interiors is one of the Best Interior Design Company in Chennai, part of Lakshmi Groups, prides itself in being a one-of-a-kind interior styling solution through its modern and trendy products which have beautiful finishes and utilitarian features.

We offer more than just an interior designing solution… We bring to you customized, turn-key concepts that completely transform interior looks into one that shows elegance, modernity, and comfort.
